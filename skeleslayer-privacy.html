SkeleSlayer — Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 10, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Maddy Van ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with the VR game SkeleSlayer ("the App"). This policy applies to all users worldwide.

SkeleSlayer is intended for users aged 13 and older. If you are under 13, you are not permitted to use this App.

1. Information we collect

SkeleSlayer does not collect, transmit, or store personal data on external servers. The following data is processed locally on your device only:

  • Gameplay progress and scores (stored locally on-device)
  • Settings and preferences you configure within the App (stored locally on-device)
  • Controller input and motion data used solely to operate gameplay and provide users with analytics.

We do not collect your name, email address, location, payment information, account credentials, or any other personally identifying information.

Please note that the Meta Quest platform may independently collect device-level data (such as device identifiers and usage analytics) in accordance with Meta's Privacy Policy. We do not control or have access to any data Meta collects at the platform level.

2. How we use and process your data

All data listed above is processed exclusively on your local device for the following purposes:

  • Saving and restoring your gameplay progress between sessions
  • Applying your in-app settings and preferences
  • Rendering gameplay in response to your controller inputs

We do not sell, share, transmit, or disclose any user data to third parties. No data is sent to our servers or any third-party service.

3. Data retention

Locally stored gameplay data (scores and settings) is retained on your device until you uninstall the App or clear its local storage through your headset's system settings. We do not retain any data on our end.

4. How to request data deletion

You can delete all data associated with SkeleSlayer at any time, regardless of your region, at no cost, and without providing a reason. To do so:

  • Uninstall SkeleSlayer from your headset. This removes all locally stored game data.

5. Children's privacy

SkeleSlayer is not directed at children under 13 and is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly permit users under 13 to access the App. Access is restricted at the platform level through Meta's age verification system. If you believe a child under 13 has accessed the App, please contact us at [YOUR_EMAIL].

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
